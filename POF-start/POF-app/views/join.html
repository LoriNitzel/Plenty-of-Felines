Welcome!

Plenty of Felines is just like online dating - but for cats! If you're looking for the purrfect friend, you've come to the right place. Once we know who you're looking for, we stalk our database full of loveable birds kitties to find your top six future furry friends.

Once you've registered, we will ask you a few simple questions. Plenty of Felines will then work its matchmaking magic. All available cats are from the Fort Collins Cat Rescue & Spay/Neuter Clinic, our wonderful community shelter.
